About Us

History Summary

Living Gate Classic School was Founded in 1994 by Rev. Mrs.Elizabeth Ochuko at Bulgion Estate, Ikorudo, Lagos State, Nigeria. The School start with about 40 students, 10 teaching staffs and 2 non-teaching staffs, the school has since then grow to more than 200 sudents and 30 staffs both teach and non-teaching. The school primary focus is on knowlede and discipline which its parents can testify to. The school is not limited to one region it also has two branchs in both Ogun State and Delta State which are located at the capitals. All Glory to God.

Our Philosophy

The LGCS is conceived as a distinctive center of learning and research; combining academic excellence with love of humanity, borne out of a God-fearing attitude, in accordance with the Pentecostal ethical behaviours, social responsibility and democratic ethos. The guiding philosophy of Living Gate Classic School is Excellence and Godliness.
